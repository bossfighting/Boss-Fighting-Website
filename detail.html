About the Commands

Here you will find detailed information about the two main commands in the Boss Fighting bot:

+advance

The +advance command allows you to progress through the different levels of your weapons, armour and potions. By advancing, you can increase the strength of your items to make them more useful.
Click Here to get a detailed explanation about the items present

+fight

The +fight command is used to initiate a boss battle. During the battle, you can use your sword, wear armor, and consume potions to help you defeat the boss.
