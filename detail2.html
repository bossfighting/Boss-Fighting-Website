Here you will find detailed information about the different items you can use in the Boss Fighting bot:

Swords

There are many types of different swords in this bot that the player will be able to use to deal more damage to the bosses. In the sword variations attack damage means the damage it does and the rank determines the quality.

Armors

Armor helps protect you from the boss's attacks. By upgrading your armor, you can increase your defense and take less damage during the fight. In the armour variations defence means the damage it blocks and the rank determines the quality.

Potions

Potions can be consumed during the battle to provide healing, when the boss damages you, you should use healing potion to restore your health. In the potion variations the heal informs the health recovery and the potion amount tells the number of potions in each level and there are no ranks in the potions.
